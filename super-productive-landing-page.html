Most People Work Harder Than They Need To. You Don't Have To.

An F1 racecar is always going to beat a Prius in a drag race. That’s obvious.

What’s less obvious is that the same thing happens at work.

The tools you use determine your speed, your output, and how much friction you feel every day.

Most people don’t choose their tools — they inherit them. Company defaults. Outdated software. “What we’ve always used.” And then they wonder why work feels harder than it should.

Here’s the uncomfortable truth: effort can’t compensate for bad tools. You can work longer hours. You can care more. But if your systems are clunky and your workflows inefficient, you’re capped.

The right tools don’t just save time — they remove friction, reduce mental load, and let you operate at a higher level.

Super Productive is a practical guide to better tools and smarter systems. Each chapter gives you a simple framework to save time and a small set of tools you can use to apply it — so you can work faster, think clearer, and get more done with less effort.

About the Author
Brendan Bockes

Hi, I’m Brendan Bockes — a web strategist, writer, and productivity nerd who’s been working remotely since 2019.

Over the years, I’ve worked with teams of all sizes, and I’ve seen the same pattern: smart, talented people either fighting their tools or failing to use the ones that could save them time.

I wrote Super Productive to help knowledge workers simplify their digital workflows, eliminate unnecessary friction, and build systems that actually support deep, meaningful work.

Being productive isn’t about cramming more into your day. It’s about working with clarity, using the right tools the right way, and creating leverage so your effort goes further.

If that resonates with you, I hope you’ll get your copy of Super Productive and see what’s possible when your tools finally work for you — not against you.
